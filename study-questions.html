Discussion and Review

Study Questions

Use these numbered questions for notes, small-group discussion, and review.

  1. What problem does Uruk face at the opening?
  2. Why do the gods create Enkidu?
  3. How does Enkidu's life in the wilderness shape him?
  4. What does Shamhat teach Enkidu about being human?
  5. Why does Enkidu lose his old place among the animals?
  6. What does the first conflict between Gilgamesh and Enkidu reveal?
  7. How does friendship change Gilgamesh's power?
  8. Why do the heroes seek the Cedar Forest?
  9. What fears do the heroes express before facing Humbaba?
  10. How does Gilgamesh define fame?
  11. What makes Humbaba's death morally complicated?
  12. Why does Gilgamesh reject Ishtar?
  13. What does the Bull of Heaven episode show about divine power?
  14. Why is Enkidu punished?
  15. How does Enkidu understand his own fate?
  16. How does Gilgamesh mourn Enkidu?
  17. Why does grief become fear?
  18. What does Gilgamesh hope Utnapishtim can teach him?
  19. What does Siduri advise?
  20. Why does Gilgamesh reject ordinary comforts?
  21. What does the journey across the waters suggest?
  22. How does Utnapishtim challenge Gilgamesh?
  23. What does the sleep test prove?
  24. Why is the Flood story important?
  25. What does the lost plant symbolize?
  26. Why does the epic end by returning to Uruk?
  27. What kind of wisdom does Gilgamesh gain?
  28. How does the poem define a meaningful human life?
