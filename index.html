Unit Throughline

Power Becomes Wisdom

Gilgamesh begins as a story about an unmatched king. It becomes a story about what that king cannot master: death.

1. Rule

Gilgamesh has power before he has restraint.

2. Friendship

Enkidu becomes the equal who can challenge, accompany, and humanize Gilgamesh.

3. Grief

Enkidu's death changes the epic from heroic adventure into a search for meaning.

4. Return

Gilgamesh returns to Uruk without immortality, but with a deeper understanding of legacy.
