Glossary

Key terms, names, and concepts for reading the epic.

Epic
A long narrative poem about heroic action and cultural values.
Uruk
Gilgamesh's city and the symbol of human achievement.
Cedar Forest
Sacred place guarded by Humbaba.
Humbaba
Guardian whose death complicates the heroes' pursuit of fame.
Utnapishtim
Flood survivor who possesses immortality by divine exception.
Mortality
The condition of being human and subject to death.
